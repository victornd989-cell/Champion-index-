## The Problem

Kenya's startup ecosystem only sees the loudest companies. The best ones — profitable, consistent, deeply embedded in the real economy — are completely invisible.

They are milling maize in Nakuru. Aggregating soybeans in Bungoma. Cold-storing flowers in Naivasha. Trading livestock in Garissa. Running fish from Lake Victoria to Nairobi before sunrise.

Nobody is backing them. Nobody is even looking for them.

**That is what Kuzana's Hidden Champions Discovery Engine fixes.**

---

## What We Built

A full AI-powered discovery platform with 5 components:

### 🤖 1. AI Discovery Agent
Searches real web sources in real time:
- Kenya business directories and registries
- Google Maps reviews (longevity and customer signals)
- Government tender award records
- News archives (to filter out already-known businesses)
- Supply chain and B2B supplier listings

The agent produces a **full company profile** for every business it finds.

### 🔍 2. The TechCabal Test
Every business is automatically checked against startup media. If it has been covered by TechCabal, Disrupt Africa or attended a pitch event — it is flagged. We only want businesses the ecosystem has genuinely missed.

### 🏆 3. Top 50 Tracker
A living database of Kenya's best Hidden Champions. Each profile includes:
- Founder background and story
- Business model and how they make money
- Revenue estimate (triangulated from public signals)
- Growth indicators
- What makes them exceptional
- Weaknesses and risks
- How to contact them
- Relationship status: Found → Contacted → Meeting Booked → Invited → In Network

### 📱 4. Field Scout Tool
A mobile-friendly form for on-the-ground discovery. A scout fills in what they observe. The AI researches the rest and builds the full profile automatically. Works in any market, any county, any sector.

### 🤝 5. Refer and Earn Network
Any Kenyan can register as a scout, get a personal referral code, and earn **Ksh 50,000** when Kuzana invests in a business they referred. This turns every accountant, SACCO leader, market trader and agrovet owner into a discovery agent.

## The Discovery Methodology

A repeatable 5-step process anyone can follow:

```
STEP 1 — FIND
Where we look: Google Maps, business directories, tender awards,
physical markets, SACCO networks, supply chain backwards from retailers.

STEP 2 — FILTER (The Hidden Test)
TechCabal test, VC test, pitch event test.
If it has been covered — it does not qualify.

STEP 3 — VERIFY
Public signal checks: longevity, customer retention,
revenue proxies (fleet size, staff, warehouse, reviews).

STEP 4 — PROFILE
Full company profile: founder, model, revenue,
growth signals, weaknesses, Kuzana fit, contact strategy.

STEP 5 — TRACK
Relationship management from Found to In Network.
Top 10 founders personally invited to Kuzana.
```
