# Kuzana — Founder Outreach Messages
### 10 personalised messages ready to send

## How to use these

- Send via **WhatsApp** (highest open rate in Kenya)
- Best time to send: **Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am**
- Keep it short — these founders are busy people
- Do NOT mention investment in the first message
- Wait 3 days before following up
